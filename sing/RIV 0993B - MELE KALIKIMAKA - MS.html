OPENER
Circle Left
Mele Kalikimaka is the thing to say
On a bright Hawaiian Christmas day
Boys Star by Right one time around
Allemande Left that corner and weave that ring around
Here we know that Christmas will be green and bright
Do sa do your partner and Promemade tonight
Mele Kalikimaka is Hawaii's way, To say Merry Christmas to you

FIGURE 1
Heads promemade and travel half way round
come down the middle and square thru go 4
All the way around and do the Right and left thru
then veer left and ferris wheel
Centers Pass thru and swing and promemade
The sun to shine by day and all the stars at night
Mele Kalikimaka is Hawaii's way, To say Merry Christmas to you
